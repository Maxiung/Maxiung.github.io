PROJECT LOG // BIOMIMETIC ROBOT HAND

Build Log

2026-01 — WEEK 1
Concept & Initial Research
Kinematics Biomimetics

Started studying gecko morphology and dexterous robotic hand designs. Defined the core goal: a tendon-driven hand that prioritizes compact form factor over brute force.

2026-01 — WEEK 2
Fusion 360 Design Iterations
Fusion 360 CAD

Moved away from clumsy motor-link structures. Redesigned joints with a motor + tendon approach — lighter, more biomimetic, better range of motion. Every dimension is a deliberate engineering decision.

2026-01 — WEEK 3
Electronics & First Wiring
ESP32-C3 Soldering Power Management

Set up the ESP32-C3 as the main controller. Learned hard lessons about voltage stability — ruined one board during soldering. Added decoupling capacitors and improved the power rail design.
