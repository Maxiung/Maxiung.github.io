Local TTS & Engineering Challenges

GPT-SoVITS Streaming Pipeline Modal Deployment Python

I started by brainstorming a voice for UU. What began as a simple runonetime.py script quickly evolved into a complex engineering task. I faced issues with overlapping audio and high latency, leading me to rewrite the entire system into a streaming pipeline.

Through four iterations of model training and experiments with background tray execution and SSE monitoring, I learned a crucial lesson: Community benchmarks don't always justify a switch. The system is now in a "usable and evolving" state, including a successful test deployment to Modal.

Robotics & Biomimetic Design

Fusion 360 ESP32-C3 Kinematics Tendon-driven

In January, my focus shifted toward building a biomimetic robot. I dived into MITx 6.002x, Linear Algebra, and Robotics Kinematics. The hands-on part was messy—I even ruined an ESP32 during soldering—but it taught me invaluable lessons about power management and voltage stability.

My design process in Fusion 360 saw a major breakthrough: moving away from clumsy motor-links to a motor + tendon-driven structure inspired by gecko morphology and dexterous robotic hands. I'm starting to think like an engineer—where every dimension is a decision.
